Anchors

Anchors are used to increase the length of messages. There are two types of anchors: Detail Anchor and Length Anchor

Detail Anchor - affects all AI model, motivating AI to write longer messages. Appends Elaborate speaker to the character

Length Anchor - affects the character played by the AI by motivating him to write longer messages. Looks like Writing style: very long messages to a message, 8 deep

Anchors Order sets the location of anchors in the promt, the first anchor in the order is much higher and has less influence than second.

The second in order anchor is turned on after 8 messages, because the first anchor is enough when there are few messages.

Sometimes some AI models may not perceive anchors correctly or the AI model already generates sufficiently long messages, so for these cases, to disable anchors, you just need to uncheck the boxes.


*It is automatically disabled in Pygmalion, since Pygmalion already generates long enough messages
